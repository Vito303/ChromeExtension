Radio SLO is a great extension app that allows you to select and play your favorite online radio in the Google Chrome browser background. This feature allows you to surf the internet and listen to the online radio simultaneously in one window.
Are you tired of looking for online radios, and you even feel annoyed when you have to open multiple browser windows just because you want to listen to the online radio and browse simultaneously? And then, when the connection is lost or the streaming is interrupted, it can be quite time consuming to search for the window, where the radio is opened and refresh/reload the live stream.
This app delivers a simple solution! It allows you to play your favorite radio by simply clicking on the play button, and the selected radio station is now playing in the browser background allowing you to browse within one browser window.
 
So far:
· Selected radio stations
· Play and Stop button
· Volume UP / Volume DOWN button
· Refresh button
